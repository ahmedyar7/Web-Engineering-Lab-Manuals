Breakfast

Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients: Flour, milk, eggs, blueberries, sugar, baking powder.

Instructions: Mix dry and wet ingredients separately. Combine gently. Fold in blueberries. Cook on a hot griddle until golden brown.

Lunch

Classic Cheeseburger

Ingredients: Ground beef, cheese slices, lettuce, tomato, buns, pickles.

Instructions: Form beef into patties. Season with salt and pepper. Grill for 4 minutes per side. Add cheese. Assemble on bun with toppings.

Dinner

Hearty Beef Stew

Ingredients: Beef chuck, carrots, potatoes, onions, beef broth, thyme.

Instructions: Sear beef chunks. Sauté vegetables. Add broth and herbs. Simmer on low heat for 2 hours until meat is tender.

Dessert

Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients: Dark chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, flour.

Instructions: Melt chocolate and butter. Whisk sugar and eggs. Combine. Bake in ramekins at 400°F for 12 minutes.
